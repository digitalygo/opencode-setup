# Streamlined checkout experience

## Purpose & Value

Enable clients to complete purchases quickly and confidently with minimal friction. This expectation reduces cart abandonment, increases conversion rates, and builds trust through transparent pricing and clear progress indicators.

## Expected Outcomes

### Primary outcome

The system enables purchase completion in three steps or fewer with clear visibility into order total, shipping options, and payment status.

### Key behaviors

- **Transparent pricing**: Full order cost including taxes, shipping, and fees visible before payment entry
- **Flexible payment**: Support for preferred methods including cards, digital wallets, and buy-now-pay-later options
- **Clear progress**: Users always know which step they are on and what remains to complete the purchase
- **Error recovery**: Helpful guidance when information is missing or incorrect, without data loss

### User segments

- **New users**: Guided experience with account creation optional, guest checkout available
- **Returning users**: Pre-filled information, one-click purchase option when desired
- **Mobile users**: Touch-optimized interface with minimal typing required

## Success Criteria

- [ ] Users complete checkout in under 2 minutes on average
- [ ] Cart abandonment rate decreases by 15% after implementation
- [ ] Users can complete purchase as guest without creating account
- [ ] Order total is visible and accurate before payment step
- [ ] Error messages clearly indicate what needs correction
- [ ] Form data persists when users navigate back or refresh
- [ ] Confirmation page displays within 3 seconds of payment acceptance
- [ ] Users receive email confirmation within 5 minutes of order placement

## Out of Scope

- Subscription management and recurring billing
- Loyalty point redemption during checkout
- International shipping with customs calculations
- Split payments across multiple cards
